Virtual Reality Direct Marketing

ABOUT JIVA


Jiva is a virtual reality content creation agency, specializing in providing immersive storytelling in the fastest and most efficient manner imaginable.


We make it possible for any demographic to enjoy exceptional virtual reality content at the push of a button.

SERVICES


PRE-PRODUCTION


Both creative and technological consulting are provided during this stage to formulate a vision for the final product, and plan to execute. Custom solutions are developed to ensure all goals, messaging, and requirements are aligned.

PRODUCTION


We go to great lengths to accomplish each individual project, no matter the challenges and no matter the scale. An extended network of some of the most accomplished cinematographers, drone pilots, and time lapse specialists are available to support our in-house team in capturing 360 imagery using a multitude of techniques.

POST-PRODUCTION


All elements related to stitching, editing, color grading, and sound design are provided to create an exceptional final product. It should be noted a great deal of time and effort is required in VR post production, so expectations should be 2-3 times the requirements of typical 2D post-production.

DELIVERY


The manufacturing, customization, and branding of Head Mounted Displays (HMDs) is completed in conjunction with production. After the final product is completed it is pre-loaded on the headsets and ready for delivery.
